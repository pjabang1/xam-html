Welcome to Premier Tree Services

Premier Tree Services is a firm of arboricultural contractors based in the North East of England. Premier specialise in all aspects of commercial and domestic tree care, using the latest techniques and equipment to carry out high quality tree work for our clients, large and small.

Premier Tree Services operate throughout the North of England including North Yorkshire, Cumbria, North East and the Scottish Borders on smaller jobs and nationwide on larger projects.

Premier Tree Services offer a wide range of tree services to both the public and private sector including:

  • Tree surgery
  • Tree inspections, tree reports and site surveys
  • Tree removal and tree dismantling
  • Hedge cutting and hedge reduction
  • Site clearance
  • Stump grinding
  • Environmental consultancy
  • Woodland management
  • Woodchip mulch, fuel grade woodchip, firewood and timber for furniture

Premier Tree Services provide a 24 hour emergency call out service and have £5 million public liability insurance. Premier Tree Services is a professional, reliable firm with a capable, well trained and enthusiastic workforce.
